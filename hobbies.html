Different cultures and use the same ingredients in such drastically different ways. I love to explore how different food combinations go together. Aside from eating, experimenting is the best part of cooking. Here are some of my favorite dishes:

Spaghetti & Meatballs, Asian Noodles, Red Beans & Rice, Birria Tacos
